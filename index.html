Tardigrade

A tardigrade is a microscopic invertebrate animal that belongs to the phylum Arthropoda (insects, crustaceans, etc.). Tardigrades are often called water bears because they have eight legs and bear resemblance to bears. They are also known as moss piglets because they look similar to mosses. Tardigrades are commonly referred to as “water bears” because of their ability to survive in extreme conditions.
